From the manufacturer

Run Longer with 4100mAh Battery

We pride ourselves on creating smartphone powerhouses that pack high-capacity batteries without compromising on great design. It's a challenge that our designers and engineers take on every day. For Redmi 4, our team has gone beyond the extra mile to carefully plan the design, do in-depth battery research, and implement system-level optimizations to present a powerhouse that excels in both battery life and design. The 4100mAh powerhouse is able to run up to 18 days on standby mode and up to 2 days with heavy duty usage.

Read more



Qualcomm Snapdragon 435 Octa-Core Processor

We're obsessed with providing you an extended, fluid experience on Redmi 4. The 4100mAh powerhouse is fueled by the Snapdragon 435 octa-core processor that boasts better performance and consumes less power compared to its predecessor. Now you can play your favorite mobile games all-day, every day without experiencing any delays.

Get a Grip

Size matters. Redmi 4 comes with a 12.7cm (5) HD display, a size that sits on your hand snugly. Our designers have gone a step further to improve the ergonomics, dressing it up in a beveled metal frame with tapered edges that rests comfortably in your palm, even during marathon gaming or movie sessions. It is topped with a front 2.5D curved glass that feels delightfully smooth when you tap or swipe your fingers across the screen.

Shoot Like a Pro with 13MP Rear Camera

Redmi 4s 13MP rear camera comes with PDAF for 0.3s fast focusing resulting in crisp and vivid images. It also has a whole range of built-in camera functions that help you take effortlessly beautiful panoramic and clear night shots. You can even apply our 36 built-in smart beauty profiles and whole range of filters in real-time to enhance your photographs.

Read more



Beautifully Finished

The Redmi 4 is encased in a beautiful metal frame that is made of anodised aluminium. The back enclosure has gone through a diamond cutting process that produces two bright strips, giving an additional brilliant sparkle to the entire phone.

Unlocks Fast with Fingerprint Sensor

Always in too much of a rush for password unlocking? Let your fingers do the unlocking. Redmi 4 comes with a fingerprint sensor that allows you to quickly unlock and access your private profile and files, fast.

Universal Remote Control

Control your TV using your phone to watch your favorite channels and programs. Redmi 4 comes with an in-built IR blaster which can control TVs, set-top boxes, ACs, DVD players and many more devices making your day to day life so convenient.

Dual Apps

Two is better than one. Be amazed with our native Dual Apps feature, which allows you to create and sign into dual accounts for any app. That includes WhatsApp, Facebook, and more!

Your Private Second Space

Use different passcodes or fingerprints to access different profiles on your Redmi 4, each with its own wallpaper, apps, files, and photos. Perfect for times when you want to show off your phone without compromising your privacy.

Faster Networking with 4G

Want to stay connected, even on-the-go? Redmi 4 supports 4G networks and VoLTE calling. So now you get to enjoy super-fast data speeds and high quality voice calling even when you're out and about.
